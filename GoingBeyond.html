What I offer:

  • Developmental Editing/Manuscript Review: Full read-through of your manuscript with in-line comments/notes suggesting ways to strengthen the manuscript prior to copy editing and proof reading. 3 cents per word.

  • Copy editing/content editing/proofreading: Full read-through of manuscript with in-line comments, focusing on the technical aspects of the manuscript like grammar, spelling, and punctuation; content editing, focusing on the overall structure, clarity, and flow of the content; and proofreading to check for consistency and accuracy and throughout. 3.5-5 cents per word, depending on size/manuscript type.
