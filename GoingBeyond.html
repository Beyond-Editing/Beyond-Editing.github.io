Expand your limits and take your writing beyond

What I offer:

  • Developmental Editing/Manuscript Review: A full read-through of your manuscript with in-line comments/notes suggesting ways to strengthen the manuscript prior to copy editing and proofreading.
    3 cents per word, depending on the size and manuscript type.

  • Copy editing/content editing/proofreading: A full read-through of your manuscript with in-line comments, focusing on the technical aspects of the manuscript like grammar, spelling, and punctuation; content editing, focusing on the overall structure, clarity, and flow of the content; and proofreading to check for consistency and accuracy throughout.
    3.5-5 cents per word, depending on the size and manuscript type.
